The World of Pokémon!

Join us in exploring the world of Pokémon! This internationally beloved game is a staple of gamers everywhere and our project explores the game, highlighting the history,characters and territories of this complex game using our website.

Check out the drop-down menu for our specialized charts highlighting information such as character background, types and special powers, as well as territories. And check our chart of the day highlighted below.

Curious to learn more about each character? Each month we highlight a fan-favorite character to help you play the game with more knowledge. And if you're in need of regular updates, we feature the latest news and happenings.

A great tool for beginners and advanced users of Pokémon!
